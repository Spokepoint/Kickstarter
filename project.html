Finding the Right Goal

Higher goals come with decreased chance of success, but some categories have different definitions of what a really high goal would be.

When choosing a goal, keep in mind all the fees beyond getting your dream off the ground. Kickstarter will take 5% of your earnings, Amazon will take 3% and you'll also need to account for backer rewards. Projects that raised double or more over their goal are the exception.
