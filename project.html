Finding the right Goal

Higher goals come with decreased chance of success, but some categories have different definitions of what a really high goal would be.

When choosing a goal, keep in mind all the fees beyond getting your dream off the ground. Kickstarter will take 5% of your earnings, Amazon will take 3% and you'll also need to account for backer rewards.

Goals between 1,000-9,999 have the largest chance of success.



Finding the right Length

Longer campaigns show less confidence and allow a longer period of time for the dreaded, dead zone of kickstarters.
